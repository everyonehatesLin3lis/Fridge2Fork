# 30-Second Demo Script

1. Double-click `run.bat` — it starts everything (venv, dependencies, Ollama, webapp).
2. Step 1: "Sup! What are we cooking with today?" — snap fridge photos, type products, or skip for staples.
3. Step 2: pick the occasion — breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks, or meal prep.
4. Step 3: pick the vibe — quick & easy, healthy, high protein, budget, or comfort food.
5. Step 4: quick details — portions, time, allergies, diet, tools — then "Cook up my recipes!".
6. Pause on the recipe cards: point out prep/cook time, portions, the prominent
   **estimated kcal per portion**, "you already have / you might need", and the
   numbered plain-language steps.
7. Say: "The app handles the meal-idea thinking, so I can stop deciding and
   start cooking."
8. Show "Behind the scenes" and name the four specialists: Vision,
   Constraints, Recipe Planner, and Final Recipe. Clarify that Hermes is the
   deterministic orchestrator, not a fifth agent.
9. Optional closing: add a product in chat or press "Different recipes" to show
   the cards refreshing without restarting the flow.
